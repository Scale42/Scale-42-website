Public statement on strategic partnership

Announcement 29 April 2024 • By Jamie Stewart

Updated: 1 May 2024

GIG Ltd and Scale42 are pleased to announce they are in advanced discussions for the creation of a joint venture focused on the development of integrated digital and energy infrastructure including sub-sea data cables and data centres.

Scale42's Nordic Leadership

Scale42, led by the founders of Hydrokraft (now Nscale — Europe's largest hyperscaler), brings extensive experience in data centre development, with a proven track record and a pipeline exceeding one gigawatt across the Nordics. This includes the recently announced 100MW flagship project in Finland.

GIG's Infrastructure Expertise

GIG is a leader in energy transmission and related infrastructure including the Atlantic Super Connection between Iceland and the UK.

Strategic Vision

This strategic partnership marks a major step toward delivering our shared vision of being a leader in integrated digital and energy infrastructure to support the growing demands for high-capacity, sustainable and connected AI infrastructure.

The collaboration represents the convergence of two industry leaders, combining Scale42's proven data centre development capabilities with GIG's extensive energy transmission and subsea infrastructure expertise.

Together, we are positioning ourselves at the forefront of the next generation of digital infrastructure, designed specifically to meet the unprecedented demands of AI workloads while maintaining the highest standards of sustainability and performance.
